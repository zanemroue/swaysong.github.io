Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2025

Sway ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use the Sway mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our App, including:

(a) Personal Information: Information that can identify you such as your name, email address, phone number, and profile information;

(b) Usage Information: Information about your interaction with our App including songs shared, voting history, and engagement metrics;

(c) Device Information: Information about your mobile device including device ID, operating system, browser type, and IP address;

(d) Location Information: General location information based on IP address or more precise location if you grant permission;

(e) Music Preferences: Information about your music preferences based on your song selections and voting patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

(a) Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features;

(b) Process and complete transactions;

(c) Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages;

(d) Respond to your comments and questions;

(e) Personalize your experience and deliver content relevant to your interests;

(f) Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities;

(g) Detect, prevent, and address fraud and other illegal activities;

(h) Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

(a) Service providers who perform services on our behalf;

(b) Other users, as part of the normal functioning of the App (e.g., your username and songs you share);

(c) In response to a legal request if we believe disclosure is required by law;

(d) To protect the rights, property, or safety of the App, our users, or the public.

4. Third-Party Music Services

Our App integrates with third-party music services like Spotify and Apple Music. When you connect your third-party music service account, we receive information about your music preferences and listening history from these services. The information we receive depends on your privacy settings with these services and their privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information.

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

(a) Access copies of your personal information;

(b) Request correction of inaccurate information;

(c) Request deletion of your personal information;

(d) Object to or restrict processing of your data;

(e) Data portability.

8. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App prior to the changes becoming effective.

9. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: zane@swaysong.com
